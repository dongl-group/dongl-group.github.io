Welcome to our Homepage!

    We are one of the research groups in MIIT Key Laboratory of Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence, College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. Our group currently has more than 10 Master students and several undergraduate students under the research guidance of Dr. Dong Liang. Our research interests include computer vision, machine learning, pattern recognition, computational photography, and human-computer interaction theory and application systems.
    We have listed our published papers in recent years below for your review.
