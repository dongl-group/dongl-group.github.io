Welcome to our Homepage!

    We are one of the research groups in MIIT Key Laboratory of Pattern Analysis and Machine Intelligence, College of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics. Our group is under the research guidance of Dr. Dong Liang (梁栋). Our research interests include computer vision, machine learning, pattern recognition, computational photography, and human-computer interaction theory and application systems.

    我们是南京航空航天大学计算机科学与技术学院模式分析与机器智能（工信部）重点实验室的研究小组之一。我们在梁栋老师的指导下，研究人工智能、计算摄影以及人机交互理论及其交叉应用系统。
